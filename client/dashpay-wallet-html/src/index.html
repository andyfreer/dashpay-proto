Welcome to the first cryptocurrency with decentralized access from any device.

  • Connect directly to the Dash P2P network from your browser or mobile device.
  • Censorship-proof as there's no centralized server to shutdown or block
  • Cheaper than PayPal and no one can freeze your funds or demand personal info
  • Buy and sell online without verification or centralized payment processors.
  • Clear your transaction history and keep your online transactions private.
  • Connect & Pay friends by name, no confusing addresses or lost change.
  • Decentralized-SPV access across 3,500 Masternodes hosted by Dash users.
  • Masternodes serve web requests in self-regulating quorums that can't be faked.
  • Downloadable HTML wallets that connect only to Dash's P2P network.
  • Hosted HTML wallets for signing in and connecting using your favourite social app
  • Access using Tor for total end-to-end online payments anonymity.
